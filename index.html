Welcome to Vasistha Foundation and Educational Trust

Education

Empowering Through Education

We provide inclusive learning, career guidance, and skill development for underserved children and youth to unlock brighter futures.

Health

Health Awareness & Hygiene

Promoting community health with hygiene campaigns, nutrition drives, and access to primary medical awareness and resources.

Agriculture

Sustainable Farming Support

We help farmers adopt eco-friendly practices, access training, and improve yields through sustainable agriculture programs.

Environment

Protecting Our Planet

Creating awareness on climate change, promoting tree planting, and encouraging youth-led environmental stewardship.
