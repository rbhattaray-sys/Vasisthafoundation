Current Openings

Education Program Coordinator

Education & Outreach

Full-time Field Work 2+ Years Exp

Lead educational initiatives in rural communities, develop curriculum, and coordinate with local schools to improve learning outcomes.

Key Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Education or related field
  • Experience in community education programs
  • Fluency in Odia and English
  • Strong communication and leadership skills

Agricultural Development Officer

Agriculture & Environment

Full-time Rural Focus 3+ Years Exp

Support farmers with sustainable farming practices, introduce new techniques, and promote environmental conservation methods.

Key Requirements:

  • Degree in Agriculture or related field
  • Knowledge of sustainable farming practices
  • Experience working with farming communities
  • Understanding of local crops and climate

Youth Development Counselor

Skill Development

Part-time Mentoring 1+ Years Exp

Provide career guidance and skill development training to young people, helping them explore opportunities and build confidence.

Key Requirements:

  • Psychology or Social Work background preferred
  • Experience in youth counseling or mentoring
  • Excellent listening and communication skills
  • Passion for youth development
